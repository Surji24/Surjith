## Key Notes

### Bit

A Bit is the smallest unit of information in classical computing.

Values:

0 or 1

### Qubit

A Qubit is the fundamental unit of information in quantum computing.

Unlike a bit, a qubit follows the principles of quantum mechanics.

### Classical Computer

Uses Bits.

### Quantum Computer

Uses Qubits.
