# Chapter 01 - Why Quantum Computing Exists

## Topics Covered

- What is Classical Computing?
- What is a Bit?
- Limitations of Classical Computing
- Why Quantum Computing Exists
- Cyber Security Impact

## Key Notes

### Bit

A bit is the smallest unit of information in classical computing.

Values:

0 or 1

### Why Quantum Computing?

Some problems are extremely difficult for classical computers:

- Cryptography
- Optimization
- Drug Discovery
- Quantum Simulations

### Cyber Security Impact

Future quantum computers may impact:

- RSA
- ECC
- Diffie-Hellman

This is why Post-Quantum Cryptography is important.
